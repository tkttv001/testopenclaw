# Estimator Quote Lean

## Quote structure
- Package: Basic | Pro | Custom
- Price
- Timeline
- Includes
- Excludes
- Delivery conditions

Rules:
- Do not invent hidden scope.
- Keep legal/ops terms concise.
- If deadline unrealistic, add one fallback plan.
